I. Course Information

Course Title and Number: Advanced Fine Arts FA101

Course Description: A comprehensive study of visual and performing arts including painting, sculpture, and digital media.

Prerequisites: Intro to Fine Arts or Instructor Approval

Course Goals/Learning Objectives: Students will create original artworks, critique peer work, and analyze art history connections.

Required Materials: Sketchbook, Drawing Pencils, Adobe Creative Suite

Course Schedule: Weekly modules released in Canvas; major projects due bi-weekly

II. Class Policies

Academic Integrity: Plagiarism will result in disciplinary action.

Attendance Policy: Attendance is required for live sessions unless excused.

Late Work Policy: 10% deduction per day, no submissions after 7 days.

Classroom Expectations: Respect others, participate actively, stay engaged.

Communication Protocols: Email or Canvas inbox preferred.

Technology Use: Chromebooks or laptops required for studio work and submission.

III. Grading

Grading Breakdown:

  • Projects – 40%
  • Participation – 20%
  • Quizzes – 15%
  • Final Exam – 25%

Grading Scale:

  • A: 90–100%
  • B: 80–89%
  • C: 70–79%
  • D: 60–69%
  • F: Below 60%
